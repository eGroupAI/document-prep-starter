# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience.

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on reproducible facts and technical context.
- Welcome different experience levels and backgrounds.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks
- Sharing private data or confidential information
- Posting content that violates legal or security boundaries

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards.
